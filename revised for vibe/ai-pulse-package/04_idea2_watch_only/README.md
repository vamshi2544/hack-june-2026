# Idea 2 · ReleaseWatch Standalone

> A focused alternative pitch: just the post-deploy agent, no ReleasePilot. Sharper story for SRE-focused audiences. Less ambitious scope, faster to demo. **Same code, same data, same prompts as Idea 1 — just trims the front half.**

## When you'd pitch Idea 2 instead of Idea 1

- The jury skews ops/SRE rather than process/automation
- You want a tighter 4-minute demo instead of 6-8
- Idea 1's pre-deploy story feels too "automation-tool" and not enough "AI"
- One of the team members will pitch this audience (e.g., a separate hackathon track)

For the primary pitch on Tuesday, **stick with Idea 1**. Idea 2 exists as an alternative framing if needed.

## What's different from Idea 1

| Aspect | Idea 1 | Idea 2 |
|---|---|---|
| Scope | Pre-deploy + Post-deploy | Post-deploy only |
| Workflow steps | 17 (steps 1-17) | 7 (steps 11-17) |
| Demo length | 6-8 min | 3-4 min |
| Agents shown | ReleasePilot + ReleaseWatch | ReleaseWatch only |
| Risk packet | Generated by ReleasePilot, read by ReleaseWatch | **Manually pre-populated** in SharePoint (or pre-imported into React) before demo |
| Pitch story | "From comment to confidence" | "Production is watching itself" |
| Key value prop | End-to-end release lifecycle automation | Cross-system AI monitoring that catches what humans miss at 2 AM |

- All 4 prompts (`01_common/prompts/`) — only L3 RCA and L2 drift are actually used; risk packet prompt is unused but referenced as evidence of "this was generated upstream"

## What gets simpler

- **No Jira trigger needed** (the demo starts at "a deploy just completed")
- **No pre-deploy approval gates** (steps 5-9 skipped)
- **No Confluence page generation** (the risk packet is pre-existing, simulating that ReleasePilot already ran)
- **No CHG advancement workflow** (CHG already in Watching state)

## What stays critical

- **L3 RCA generation** — still the demo's killer moment
- **Cross-system pattern detection** — still the core insight
- **Human handoff via Adaptive Card** — still the moment that lands

## Recommended decision rule

- **Default: Idea 1.** End-to-end is more impressive and uses all the prompts.
- **Switch to Idea 2 only if:** you've lost too much time on Idea 1's pre-deploy half and want to focus the remaining hours on polishing the post-deploy story.
- **Don't try to "do both" as separate pitches.** Pick one for Tuesday and commit.
