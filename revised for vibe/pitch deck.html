The system was already broken.

Health checks pass. But a downstream service has silently stopped — no error, no alert, just a flatline no dashboard is watching for.

By the time anyone notices, it’s already too late — transactions dropped, and the customer’s messages were never sent.

The idea

Two AI teammates for the release lifecycle.

ReleasePilot
before deploy · predict & prepare

Computes the release risk, then does the manual prep — the QCPR docs, the Confluence pages, and the ServiceNow change ticket — posting every step to Teams and pausing for you to confirm.

🤝risk packet
ReleaseWatch
after deploy · detect & explain

Watches the right signals, catches the drift health checks miss, and turns a failure into a root-cause analysis with a confidence score — then raises the incident.

The handoff is the insight — the watcher arrives already briefed on what to watch for.
